Story

There are many myths about how it all started. None of them provide much confidence in believing in them. But one thing is clear: how it is discovered on Earth-351.

People say it was in the 21st century. An engineer was working late on a program when accidentally noticed something strange. He could not find the source of it and that did not make any sense. He spent days trying to debug it or find the source until he saw a pattern. He was able to decipher the code and read it. The logs were about the war and deadly battles. The more he spent time deciphering it the more he realized, the war is not in a specific place or time. It was a Multiversal War. Eager to know more he built a program to decipher the stream. The result became "The Fabric of Reality": a communicator between Worlds. His curiosity led to opening a portal to a Multiversal War, a story yet to be told.
